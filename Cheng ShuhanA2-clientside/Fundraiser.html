Disaster Relief Fund
In the face of natural disasters, each of us can be a victim. The Disaster Relief Fund is committed to providing emergency assistance in the immediate aftermath of a disaster to help people rebuild their homes and lives. Every donation you make will go directly to providing emergency relief supplies such as food, medical supplies, temporary shelter, and more. Join us in igniting a ray of hope for those in need. Your donation will bring new hope and strength to countless families.
